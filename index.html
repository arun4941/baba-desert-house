Kalakand

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder

Recipe

  1. Heat milk in a heavy-bottom pan.
  2. Cook the milk till it is reduced to half.
  3. Add the lemon juice to the pan.
  4. Keep cooking till the milk is reduced and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  5. Add the sugar and cook for a minute.
